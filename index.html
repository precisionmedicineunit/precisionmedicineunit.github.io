At the forefront of multi-omics, our expertise in genomics, proteomics, and advanced data analysis drives innovation in precision medicine. We deliver tailored solutions for healthcare professionals and researchers. This is the landing page for the pipeline development project <PMU>. This serves to provide links to documentation, code, and background information about our bioinformatic work. Most of the technical details can be found in the DOCS pages. Visit the PrecisionMedicineUnit.com homepage for links to our platforms.

To contact us about pipeline code, please visit github.com/dylanlawless project repositories. For other general contact, please vist the PrecisionMedicineUnit.com homepage.
